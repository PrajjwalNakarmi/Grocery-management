Technology and It's Impact

About Technology

Technology is constantly changing the way we live in our daily life completely. From the complex daily activities like bioengineering, rocket science, etc.to simple online shopping in e-commerce websites, we rely on technology every second of our life. In a way, technology has helped to bring the world closer and better connected among us. Technology doesn't always have to be flashy big machines or bright screens on our electronic devices. It could be as simple as a water pump or a simple pulley, telling us that technology is, in simple words, the invention of machines or groups of machines that help us do our jobs efficiently and easily.There are tons of types of technology such as Communication Technology, Entertainment Technology, BioTechnology, Robotics, Electronics, Artificial Intelligence, and much more.Electical gadgets, faster modes of communication, and transport have added so much comfort in our lives. It has brought the chance of an individual becoming maximum productible. Technology has brought a revolution in many operational fields. While it seems technology has lots of positives and benefits, there are also lots of drawbacks and negative impacts of technology which we will be discussing down below in detail.

Positive Impact Of Technology

In this modern era, we cannot live without technology.It has became a essential element of out life. And why wouldn't it be? It has so many positives in our lives. Starting with Communication Technology, probably one of the most, if not the most useful invention of technology has helped us to be connected with people from the other side of our big world.Before there was no actual proper way for us to communicate with people far from us except for letter, which are really inconvinient. But now the help of Communication Technology, it's hundred times easier to tranmit data from one place to another and you could also communicate using different social media apps like whatsapp, skype, etc. plus the new 5g makes it even faster and easier to communicate. Moreover, Japan recently, introduces an innovative prototype showcasing the immense capabilities of 6G technology.
With the help of technology, it is now very easy for a person to find any source of entertainment in the ineternet. With the increase of growth of quality of movies, video games, virtual reality(VR), modern museums,etc it is very easier for a person to be entertained at all times. Every company, every students, every parents utilizes Entertainment Technology to enjoy their free times without being bored.
One of the other advantages of technology is Automation, Technology helps people to do the repetitive and mundane tasks without much trouble. Automation helps to improve productivity and reduces errors in different industries such as manufacturing, engineering, logistics, agriculture and many more industries have benefited from automated processes, reducing the need for manual labor. This results in increased precision, consistency, and a reduction in workplace injuries as well.
Lastly Aritifical Intelligence(AI),AI offers a range of benefits to technology. It helps with automation, making workflows smoother and cutting down on mistakes. It's also great at handling big datasets fast, and make smarter choices.Plus, AI can personalize services based on what individuals like, which can really improve user experiences. Overall, AI has the potential to completely change different industries, encouraging innovation and creating new opportunities for growth.For example:AI is transforming online shopping. When we look for shoes, AI suggests styles based on our preferences. It also helps the stores stock the right items, boosting satisfaction. AI's personalization and efficiency are improving the shopping experience.

Negative Impact Of Technology

Having so much positives doesn't overlap the negatives of technology. While using Technology, there is always high chance of your data to be leaked or destroyed anytime. Those valuable datas and information, if gets in the wrong hand could be very dangerous, especially with the amount of online cyber crimes like cyber bullying, blackmailing, Cyberstalking, etc happening every second,in every corner of the world which are ignored and not considered. Another negative impact could be unemployment, we have seen seeing the trend for a lot of ears now that machines and robots being able to task faster and easier than people who made the machines. Because of this, there is high chance that the robots completely replace human being in the near future. For example: Lots of people prefer online shopping making the people not using the technology harder to run a sucessful marketplace.
People are also being less and less creative as they are being completely dependent upon technology. Becoming lazy and destroying their own health. Manipulating Digital Media, creating fake personas, fake news and much more negative inpacts exists while using technology.
Additionally, technology's rapid advancement raises fears of job displacement due to automation and artificial intelligence. It's crucial for individuals, industries, and governments to address these challenges through responsible use, regulation, and ethical considerations to ensure that the negative impacts are minimized while maximizing the benefits of technology.
